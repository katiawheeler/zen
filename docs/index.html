Zen

Mindful focus, automated.
Tranquility in your menu bar.

Zen harmonizes your calendar, focus modes, and presence—automatically enabling deep work when you're in meetings and releasing when you're free.

Download for macOS Requires macOS 13.0 or later

Capabilities

Designed for deep work

Automatic Focus Mode

Zen watches your calendar and automatically enables Focus Mode when meetings begin. When you're free, it releases—no manual toggling required.

Google Calendar Sync

Connect your Google Calendar with secure OAuth. Zen monitors your events in real-time and displays upcoming meetings right in your menu bar.

Menu Bar Native

Lives quietly in your menu bar, hidden from the dock. The breathing circle provides a gentle visual indicator of your current focus state.

Siri & Shortcuts

Full integration with Apple Shortcuts and Siri. Control your focus with voice commands or build custom automations that fit your workflow.

Coming Soon

Outlook Calendar

Connect your Microsoft Outlook calendar for seamless meeting detection. Perfect for enterprise environments and Office 365 users.

Coming Soon

Slack Status Sync

Automatically update your Slack status when Zen Mode activates. Let your team know you're in focus mode without lifting a finger.

Privacy First

Your credentials never leave your machine. Zen stores OAuth tokens securely in the macOS Keychain—the same encrypted vault that protects your system passwords. No cloud storage, no analytics, no data collection. Your calendar stays yours.

Local Only Keychain Encrypted No Cloud Storage

Getting Started

Three steps to tranquility

1

Connect Your Calendar

Sign in with Google to sync your calendar. Zen securely accesses your events to know when you're in meetings.

2

Create Focus Shortcuts

Set up two simple Shortcuts: "Zen Mode On" and "Zen Mode Off". These control your macOS Focus settings.

3

Enable Automation

Toggle on automatic mode in settings. Zen will handle the rest—activating focus during meetings and releasing when you're free.
